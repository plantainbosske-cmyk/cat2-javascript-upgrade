Documented Progress

Variety of Plantains

We have a wide variety of plantains available, including green, yellow, and ripe plantains. Our plantains are perfect for any dish, whether you're looking to make fried plantains, stews, or desserts.

Commitment to Quality

We are committed to providing our customers with the freshest and highest quality plantains in the country. Our plantains are sourced from local farmers who use sustainable farming practices to ensure that our customers receive the best possible product. Our journey began with a small plot of land and a dream to cultivate the best plantains in Kenya. Through hard work, dedication, and a commitment to sustainable farming practices, we have transformed our farm into a thriving business that provides high-quality plantains to customers across the country.

Passion, Persistence, and Plantains

Struggle to Survival of Plantains

The first plantains we planted were a struggle to grow, but with persistence and dedication, we were able to cultivate a thriving plantain farm. Our journey from struggling to survive to becoming a leading plantain farmer in Kenya is a testament to our passion for providing the best plantains to our customers.

Rain Feeding and Enriching the Plantains

Climate change has brought unpredictable weather patterns, making it challenging to maintain a consistent water supply for our plantains. However, we have adapted by implementing rainwater harvesting techniques and enriching our soil with organic compost to ensure our plantains receive the nutrients they need to thrive. Our commitment to sustainable farming practices has allowed us to continue providing high-quality plantains to our customers despite the challenges posed by climate change.
