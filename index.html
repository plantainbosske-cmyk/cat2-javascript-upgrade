About Our Farm

Focus on Organic Regenerative Agriculture

At Plantain Boss Ltd's farm our aim is to protect and guard the soils' microbiome and its Eco-System. We work to sustain its nutrition organically and grow the healthiest, prolific plantains. Our philosophy is; Feeding the Soil, Feeds the Plants and as a result, the plant Feeds Us. Our use of organic compost manure, woodash, rainwater and mulch creates a richer Soil Eco System for growth and sustainability.

Commitment to Quality

We are committed to providing our customers with the freshest and highest quality plantains in the country. We do this by staying on ground, continously monitoring, evaluating, searching for emerging best practices and making appropriate improvements to ensure we meet our market unique needs.

Our Passion and Persistence for Plantains

Rain Feeding and Soil Enrichment

Climate change has brought unpredictable weather patterns, making it a challenge to maintain consistent water supply nationwide. However, at Plantain Boss Ltd Farm we have adapted, by implementing rainwater harvesting techniques and enriching our soil with organic compost to ensure our plantains receive the right amount of moisture and nutrients they need to thrive on a year round basis. Our aim is to shift and scale to Solar Powered Irrigation systems to boost more prodcution and bring more plantain to your tables.
